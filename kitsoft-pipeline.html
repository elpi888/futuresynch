My Team AI — 5-Step Outreach Flow

01
LinkedIn Connection
Personalised note referencing summit. One sentence. Reference something specific.
Within 48 hrs
02
Value Follow-Up
Share one genuinely useful piece of content. End with an open question about their work. No product mention.
Within 5 days
03
Book the Call
30-min peer conversation. 'Sharing what we see in UK GovTech' — not a sales call. Frame as a learning exchange.
Within 1 week of reply
04
Listen & Present
Ask about their biggest digital service friction point. Then present Liquio as the answer to what they described. End with a demo offer.
On the call
05
Live Demo
20-min bespoke demo. Build a service they recognise. End: 'We can have a proof of concept running for your organisation in 6 weeks.'
Within 1 week of call
→
Proposal
Commercial terms introduced only after demo. Pilot proposal: one service, one organisation, 6 weeks.
Post-demo

Weekly Operating Cadence

Frequency Action Success Signal
Daily Check for replies. Respond within 24 hours. Send one outreach message working through contacts in tier order. Log every interaction in tracker. Zero unanswered messages. Tracker updated same day.
Monday Review pipeline. Identify contacts ready to move to next step. Send Step 03 call invitations to contacts who replied to Step 02. Prepare demo script for calls booked this week. At least 1 new call booked per week.
Friday Share one relevant piece of content with every active contact — a Liquio update, GovTech article, or case study. The 1% — one small touchpoint that keeps the relationship warm. 5+ content shares. No contact goes 7+ days without a touchpoint.
Monthly Full pipeline review. Contacts by stage. Follow-up rate. Demos delivered. Proposals submitted. Any contact stuck at the same stage for 3+ weeks — change approach. At least 1 formal proposal submitted per month.
